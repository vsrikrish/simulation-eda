Time Series and Environmental Data

\[X = \{X_t, X_{t+h}, X_{t+2h}, \ldots, X_{t+nh}}\]

More often: ignore sampling time in notation,

\[X = \{X_1, X_2, X_3, \ldots, X_n}\]
